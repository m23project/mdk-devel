Overview of the menu entries

Current drive and partition Many operations are bound to a partition (e.g. formating) or a drive (e.g. creation of a new partition). Such functions correspond to the selected drive (''Current drive'') and the selected partition (''Current partition'') that are choosen under ''Current drive and partition''.
Virtual drives If you are defining a client to be used for mass installation, you need to create templates for the harddisks here. At ''Drive name'' select the device name you would like to use, enter the size of the drive at ''Size'', and finally, click on ''Create''.
