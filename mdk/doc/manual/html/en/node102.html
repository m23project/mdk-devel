Additional information:

After adding a new client, the client will boot over the network and runs through a hardware detection sequence. Hardware data (hard disk size, partitions etc.) will be sent to the server. When the data transfer is finished the client will change its status to "yellow". You can setup your client now. To setup your client click on "Clients" "Setup" in the menu.
