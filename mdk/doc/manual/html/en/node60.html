Security

To protect your data from unwarranted access, these will be encrypted before they are sent to goos-habermann.de. For this purpose, goos-habermann.de uses an own (self-signed) certificate which needs to be imported into your browser first. To check if it has already been imported or to start importing, just click on "". Your browser will issue a warning saying that the issuer of the certificate is untrusted and ask you what to do next. You need to choose that you accept the certificate in order to render encrypted communication possible. The certificate's properties are:
