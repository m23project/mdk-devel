Changing the package priority

Packages or jobs are executed following the order of their priority (small numbers preceed higher numbers) on the client. If you would like to change the priority, select the desired packages and enter a new value for their priority into the field next to ''Change priority''. When you are finished, click on ''Change priority''. If necessary, repeat the process for further jobs/packages.
