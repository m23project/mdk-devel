Manage GPG keys

You can add new GPG keys or remove existing GPG keys to and from the m23 server with the dialog "Manage GPG keys".

  • Adding a new key:
    1. Copy the desire public GPG/PGP key with your preferred key management application to the clipboard.
    2. Paste the key into the input box on the left of "Import GPG key".
    3. Click on "Import GPG key" to complete the procedure.
  • Deleting of an existing key:
    1. Choose the key from the list you want to delete
    2. Click on "Delete" afterwards.
