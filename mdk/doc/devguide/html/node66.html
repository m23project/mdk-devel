Better naming for your files

You normally will develop your application in the /m23 directory. To make finding your files easier you should name your files like BACKUP_startpage.php. Your files should always begin with your selected prefix. Now you can find your files with: 
find /m23/ -name BACKUP*
To make a package with the found files, simply use the following script: 
for file in `find . -name BACKUP_*`
do
 files=$files" "$file
done
tar cvj -f myPackage.tb2 $files
Where you replace BACKUP_ with your package prefix and myPackage with your package name.
