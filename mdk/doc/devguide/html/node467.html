CFDiskBasic::deletePartitionJob

Description: Generates a partition removal job and adds it to the list of partition steps.
Parameter:
  • dev: selected device (e.g. hda)
  • pPart: Physical partition number, minor number in parted
  • addAtTheBeginning: Adds the job to the beginning of the partitionSteps and partitionStepsForShift arrays.
