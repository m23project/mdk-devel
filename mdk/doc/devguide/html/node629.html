CFDiskIO::delDevFromRaid

Description: Removes a device (disk or partition) from the list of devices building the RAID.
Parameter:
  • vrDisk: Virtual (internally used) RAID disk number.
  • dev: Device to remove from the RAID.
Returns:
  • true, if the device could be removed, otherwise false.
