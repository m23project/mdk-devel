CFDiskIO::doesDiskHavePartitionsLockedByRaid

Description: Checks if the disk has at least one partition that was assigned to a RAID.
Parameter:
  • vDisk: Virtual (internally used) disk number.
Returns:
  • true, if there is at least one partition that was assigned to a RAID on the disk, otherwise false.
