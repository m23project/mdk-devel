CFDiskBasic::autoPartitionDisk

Description: Automatically partitions and formats a disk.
Parameter:
  • diskDev: The disk device (e.g. /dev/hda) that should be partitionated and formated automatically.
  • minSwap: Minimal size of the swap partition in MB.
  • maxSwap: Maximal size of the swap partition in MB.
